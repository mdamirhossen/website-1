Frequently Asked Questions & Answers

What does it cost?

Our treatment costs SEK 100 / psychologist call and you make a maximum of 7 calls in total (including the initial assessment call), so the total cost will be a maximum of SEK 700.

You book psychologist calls via our app (which is completely free) by logging in securely with your Bank ID. You only pay when you choose to book a psychologist call. No other costs are added.

Does the exemption card / high-cost protection apply?

Exemption cards and high-cost protection apply with us. If you have a free card, your treatment can be free of charge. If you have reached the maximum amount of SEK 1150, the calls are free of charge if they take place within the 12-month period, your free card is valid. Should you come up with the amount for exemption card during our treatment, you can of course use it from then on.

How does your treatment work in practice?

The first thing that happens is that you are assigned a psychologist for the initial assessment interview. Should you choose to go ahead and start our treatment, the same psychologist will follow you throughout the treatment. Every week, the psychologist gives you support, feedback and tasks that you must carry out. Our treatment includes thought management, sleep routines, sleep diary & education.

Can you go the treatment at a distance?

Yes! Our entire treatment is based on it being able to easily work with your everyday life. It does not matter where you live, but all treatment and communication takes place via our app.

What is CBT and why do you use it?

CBT stands for Cognitive Behavioral Therapy and it is the form of treatment that is currently best suited, according to research and the National Board of Health and Welfare, for you with long-term sleep difficulties. Read more at 1177.se .

What are Learning to Sleep?

Learning to Sleep is a digital sleep clinic that was founded in 2009 with the ambition of offering the best digital CBT treatment for people with sleep problems. We offer site-independent and results-based CBT treatment based on multi-year research.
We are a dedicated team of psychologists and digital specialists who are all driven by a vision to change people's lives through better sleep.

Which health center do you collaborate with?

Learning to Sleep has an agreement with Vårdcentral Doktor.se Norra Djurgården, the reception is located at Bobergsgatan 91 in Norra Djurgårdsstaden. This in turn means that all visits to Learning to Sleep are registered via Vårdcentral Doktor.se Norra Djurgården. Registrations of care contacts via Vårdcentral Doktor.se Norra Djurgården will thus be visible in the patient record at 1177.
